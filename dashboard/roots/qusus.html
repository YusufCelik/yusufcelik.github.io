: see مَقْصُوصٌ, in two places. -A2- A tomb plastered with قَصّ [or gypsum]: and in like manner مُقَصَّصَةٌ applied to a city (مَدِينَة). (M, TA.)
مَقْصُوصٌ ذ
Cut, clipped, or shorn; applied to hair [&c.]; as also : (M, TA:) and to a wing; as also . (A.) مَقْصُوصُ الخَنَاحِ A bird having the wing clipped. (S.) And Having the forelocks clipped, or shorn. (Meyd, in Golius.)
