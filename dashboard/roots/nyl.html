1 نَالَ مِنْهُ ذ
He defamed him. (L, art. قبح.) -b2- نَالَ مِنْ عِرْضِهِ He defamed him. (T, K, TA.) -b3- نَال مِنْهُ He harmed, hurt, or injured him, namely, an enemy. (Mgh.) -b4- نَالَهُ بِلِسَانِهِ is coupled in the Msb, art. قرص, with أَذَاهُ; and seems plainly to signify أَصاَبَهُ, which, in this case, is the same as أَذَاهُ. And has a similar meaning. -b5- نَالَ He obtained, or attained. (S, K.) نَالَ مِنْ عَدُوِّهِ He attained [or obtained] the object of his aim, or desire, from his enemy. (Msb.) -b6- نَالَهُ, aor. يَنَالُ, It reached him; came to him; syn. وَصَلَ إِلَيْهِ. (M, art. نيل.) See also Bd, xxii. 38. -b7- نَالَ لَكَ أَنْ نَفْعَلَ, aor. يَنِيلُ; and لَكَ: see أَنَى. -b8- نَالَهُ, first. pers. نِلْتَهُ, inf. n. نَيْلٌ, He obtained it; he attained it; namely, the object of his wish, &c. (S, K, Msb, &c.)

إِنَانَةٌ ذ
The act of giving: (PS in art. نيل;) the giving a gift. (KL.) It seems properly to belong to art. نول.
نَيْلٌ ذ
(T, M, K) and (M, K) What one obtains, or acquires, (T, M, K,) of the bounty of another; like نَوَالَ. (T.)
