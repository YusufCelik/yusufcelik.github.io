And in like manner, وَجْهُ أَمْرٍ and The end, or result, of an affair, to which it leads, or tends. -b28- رَمَوْا وَجْهًا وَاحِدًا [They shot in one direction]. (M voce رِشْقٌ.) -b29- وَجْهُ الضُّحَى The first, or beginning, of the ضُحَى. (TA voce رَوْنَقٌ, q. v.) -b30- وَجْهٌ A chief of a people or party. (K.) -b31- أَتَوْا مَنْ وَجْهِهِمْ: see فَوْرٌ. -b32- وَجْهٌ i. q. طَرِيقَةٌ [meaning The mode, or manner, of a thing]. (KL.) -b34- مَا أَدْرِى مَا وَجْهُهُ I know not what is its meaning. -b35- أَخَذَ وَجْهَهَا [app. He degraded her; took away her grade: and hence he took her maidenhead: see وَجَّهْتِ سِجَافَتَهُ].
جَهَةٌ ذ
: see وَجْهٌ throughout. -b2- مِنْ جِهَةِ كَذَا In respect of, or with reference to, such a thing: and by reason, or on account, or because, of such a thing. -b3- الجِهَاتُ السِّتُ The six relative points or directions or locations; namely, above, below, before, behind, right, and left.
وِجْهَةٌ ذ
: see وَجْةٌ. -b2- A way, mode, or manner, of acting, &c.
