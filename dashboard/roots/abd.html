مُؤَبَّدٌ ذ
[Made, or rendered, perpetual]. You say, وَقَفَ أَرْضَهُ وَقْفًا مُؤَبَدًا He made his land an unalienable bequest for pious uses in perpetuity, not to be sold nor to be inherited. (T.) -b2- Also, with ة, A she-camel that is wild, and intractable, or unmanageable; syn. وَحْشِيَّةٌ مُعْتَاصَةٌ. (K.)
