طِبِّىٌّ ذ
Medical, therapeutical, or curative; of, or relating to, medical, therapeutical, or curative, treatment. (Msb.)
طَبْطَبِيَّةٌ ذ
A [kind of whip, or scourge, such as is called] دِرَّة [q. v.]: (K, TA:) because the sound that is made by its fall is like طَبْ طَبْ. (TA.)
طَبْطَابٌ ذ
A certain bird, or flying thing, (طَائِرٌ,) having large ears. (K.)
طَبْطَابَةٌ ذ
, or طِبْطَابَةٌ, (accord. to different copies of the K,) A broad piece of wood, with which one plays with the ball, (K, TA,) or with which the horseman plays with the ball. (T, TA.)
الطَّبَاطِبُ ذ
