مِحْرَاثٌ محراث
The thing (i. e. the piece of wood, or the wooden thing, TA) with which the fire is stirred (S, A, K) in the [kind of oven called] تَنُّور; (S;) as also : (K:) and مِحْرَاثُ النَّارِ the shovel (مِسْحَاة) with which the fire is stirred. (TA.) [Hence,] مِحْرَاثُ الحَرْبِ (assumed tropical:) That which [or he who] stirs up, or excites, war. (TA.) -b2- [In the present day, it signifies A plough: and (like حَرْثٌ) a ploughshare.]

and Ground ploughed up by people's treading much upon it. (T, TA.)
