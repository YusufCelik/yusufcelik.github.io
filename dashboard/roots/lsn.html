Chasteness, or perspicuity, or clearness, of speech, (S, Msb, K,) and eloquence; (Msb;) i. q. بَيَانٌ: or, as some say, the quality of speaking well: and chasteness, or perspicuity, or eloquence, of speech, and sharpness of tongue. (TA.)

of a sandal, The thing (هَنَة) projecting in the fore part thereof. (TA.) See مِسْرَدٌ. -b2- لِسَانٌ The tongue [or cock] of a balance: see مِنْجَمٌ in the S and K. -b3- لِسَانٌ In formation, news, or tidings; syn. خَبَرٌ. (S in art. علو.) See a verse cited voce عُلْوٌ.
تَلَسُّنٌ ذ
: see خَلِيَّةٌ, voce خَلِىٌّ
مُلَسَّنٌ ذ
