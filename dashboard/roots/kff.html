كِفَافَةٌ ذ
[app. a subst., not an inf. n.,] The act, or art, of sewing in the manner termed كَفٌّ; (TA;) contr. of شِلَاشَةٌ. (TA in art. شل.)
نَاقَةٌ كَافَّةٌ ذ
An old and weak she-camel. (AO, TA in art. سدم.) -b2- [مَا كَافَّةٌ The restrictive مَا, which is annexed to أَنَّ إِنَّ, &c.: so called because it restrains the particle to which it is adjoined from exercising any government.] -b3- كَافَّةً Wholly. (Bd and Jel in ii. 204.)
