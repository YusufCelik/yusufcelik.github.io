(TA, from a trad.) -b2- And with ة, applied to the kind of jar termed مُزَمَّلَة, Having a سُرَّة, meaning a perforation in the middle, in which is fixed a tube of silver or lead, whence one drinks. (Har p. 548.)
وَقَفْتُ عَلَى مُسْتَسَرِّهِ ذ
I became acquainted with his hidden, or secret, affair. (A, * TA.)
